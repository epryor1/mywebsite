Welcome to The Coffee Co.

Welcome to our cozy coffee shop. We Look forward to providing the best cup of coffee every day, just when you need it! We love serving our small community and welcoming new visitors. Check out our events page to get social. Conveniently, we also have our menu online if you want to know what you want to order before you get here. We also take online orders, so check that the area as well!

We also have a merch area that shows what tote bags of the month we have up for sale in our shop. At this time, we can't take online orders, so these are just available in the shop. They fly off the shelves. In the future we plan to include t-shirts and mugs. Both have been highly requested. Currently you can buy our Logo T-shirt in store, if you want to support us.

We make sure to sustainably source our coffee. The people who grow the coffe anre just as important as the people crafting your drink. Craft coffee is an experience from the plant to your hands.

Inside our shop we have a room for meetings if desired. Just tell our barista at the front counter when you would like to schedual your meeting or if the room is available go ahead and use it.

So, come in for a visit or meet us every morning for your daily latte, we would love to make your perfect cup of coffee, to make your day better. If you don't love your drink, please tell us so we can try again! We want it to be perfect.

Be sure to check out our Events Page!

Here is a quick video overview of current events! We believe in creating a strong community. When we befriend our neighbors, we create a stronger community. So, in an effort to create those friendships, we host many monthly and weekly events. We have game nights and book clubs and trivia nights. There are some community events we attend, so make sure you stay up-to-date and come visit! To find more details go to our events page. We also have a feedback area on our contacts page, so if you have ideas for events we should include, we can gauge interest and see about planning a new event. Our Board Game Nights came from a suggestion, so the possibilities are endless. Maybe tell us your favorite movie and we can build a trivia night around it.
