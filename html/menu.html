Here is a little bit of information about the menu. Sometimes coffee shops can be intimidating if you are unfamiliar with them, so we want to make your experience as great as possible. Every cup is made with care And attention so that each one is special. Come in today for your custom personalized cup of coffee. Coffee is great warm and cold and sunny and rainy.

We can make any coffee or tea drink you could think of, but this is our base menu with a little bit about our prices.

Of our base coffee drinks, we have a cappuccino, latte, double espresso, americano, and a macchiato. Typically, the cappuccino and americano don't have flavors added in, but if you would like the extra flavor we can add that in for you. Also, a double espresso is just two plain shots of espresso.

We have a great syrup selection, and we make all of them in-house. Some of our seasonal flavors were so popular we kept them all year. Like out Gingerbread and Mint. Our mixtures of flavors can get you a nice basic vanilla latte, a cozy gingerbread latte, or a refreshing mint flavor. Try them all! If there are any flavors, we don't carry please fill out the feedback section, we would love to hear from you.

We have several milk alternatives; we have almonds and oat. The dairy products we have are two percent, half and half, and whole milk. these can be added to any drink to replace whole milk.

For our specialty drinks:

Brown Sugar Cinnamon: A lovely, sweet drink with brown sugar and our cinnamon syrup. Typically served with whole milk but add any substitutions you would like.

London Fog: A London fog is a tea latte. The tea used is earl grey with a pump of vanilla syrup. This drink feels like a warm cozy sweater on a cold day.
