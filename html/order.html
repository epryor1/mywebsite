Quick tips for Ordering:

Make sure you fill your name in on your order. It wonâ€™t let you order without a name, but just in case. Double-check your drink size. If you have any questions on the menu, our phone number is on the side of the page, or on our contact page. We can also assist you with an order over the phone. Please make sure to pick the correct day and time you can pick it up. You wouldn't want an old drink. If you cannot pick up immediately and are ordering ahead you can use the date and time menu to let us know when you will be picking up your drink. We are not responsible for unpicked-up drinks. When you come to pick up your drink, go to the register, and the cashier will help you check out and get you your beverage.

To fill out the form, you need to put your name. Then pick your preferred size, small, medium, or large. Then pick your type of drink. Cappuccino, Americano, Latte, Double Espresso, Macchiato. (Note: some drinks are classically without syrup. We will still add syrup if you prefer, but we to see which drinks traditionally get the syrup to check out our menu.) Then choose your add-ins. In our selection of syrups we have vanilla, caramel, mocha, cinnamon, mint, ginger, brown sugar, pumpkin spice, nutmeg and french vanilla. So as you can see we have many to choose from, if we don't have your favorite, drop us a comment on our feedback area so we can accommodate you. Then choose your preferred milk. We have many options as well as non-dairy substitutes such as almond and oat. We also have half and half, whole, and two percent milk. Then you choose if you prefer your drink hot or iced. Lastly, fill in your necessary date and time to guarantee it will be waiting for you.

If for any reason you need to cancel your order, please let us know as soon as possible. Please give us a call and we can edit your time for you. That way we can make sure your drink if fresh and ready when you are. It typically takes us an average of ten minutes to prepare your drink, so take that into account.

Again thank you so much to our valued customers. We love that we can make your morning more efficient with this service. All we ask is that we are treated with courtesy as we prepare your order. And please pick up your orders.
